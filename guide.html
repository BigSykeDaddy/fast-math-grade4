FAST Math Study Guide (Kid-Friendly Explanations)

This page gives **easy, short explanations** you can use together for each problem type. Once you understand, go back to the Practice Page.

1. Whole Numbers

Rounding 47,306 to the nearest thousand
Look at the hundreds digit (3). If it’s 5 or more, round up. If it’s 4 or less, stay.
👉 3 is small, so we stay: 47,000.
Think: 0–4 “stay on the floor,” 5–9 “climb the line.”
Multiplying 234 × 12
Break it into easy chunks:
- 234 × 10 = 2,340
- 234 × 2 = 468
Add them: 2,340 + 468 = 2,808.
Tip: Cover the zero to do ×10, then just put the zero back.
Dividing 504 ÷ 8
Ask: “How many 8’s fit in 50?” That’s 6 (because 8×6=48). Remainder 2.
Bring down the 4 → 24. Now 8 goes into 24 three times.
Answer = 63.

2. Fractions & Decimals

Equivalent Fractions (3/4 = ?/8)
Multiply top and bottom by the same number. 4×2=8, so 3×2=6.
Answer = 6/8.
Think: Make the bottom bigger, the top grows too.
Adding Fractions (3/8 + 2/8)
Same bottom = just add the tops. 3+2=5. Answer = 5/8.
Multiply Fraction × Whole (3/5 × 4)
Do top × whole. 3×4=12 → put over 5. Answer = 12/5 or 2 2/5.
Think: four groups of 3/5 = 12/5.
Convert 45/100 to Decimal
Say it as “forty-five hundredths.” That’s 0.45.

3. Measurement & Conversions

6 feet → inches
1 foot = 12 inches. So 6 × 12 = 72 inches.
Half a gallon → quarts
1 gallon = 4 quarts. Half = 2 quarts.
1.5 hours → minutes
1 hour = 60 min. Half an hour = 30 min. Total = 90 min.

4. Geometry & Angles

Right angle (90°)
Makes an “L” corner. Like the corner of a piece of paper.
120° angle
Bigger than 90°, but less than a straight line. That’s an obtuse angle.
Quadrilateral with 2 pairs of parallel sides
Like opposite sides of a window frame. Answer = Parallelogram.

5. Data & Probability

Mode of 3, 5, 5, 6, 6, 6, 2
Mode = most common. 6 shows up most → Answer = 6.
Range of 2, 6, 4, 9
Biggest minus smallest = 9 − 2 = 7.
Probability of even on a die
Die has 6 sides. Evens = 2,4,6 → that’s 3 out of 6. Simplify to 1/2 (same as 50%).
Think: half the time you’ll roll even.
